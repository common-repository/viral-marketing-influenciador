== Description ==

= Boost your sales with influencer marketing! =

The **Viral Marketing Influenciadores app** is the ultimate solution to boost your sales through influencer marketing. With it, you can:

* **Generate personalized discount coupons** for each influencer.
* **Monitor sales metrics** directly on the platform.

= Why use Viral.com.br? =

* **Discover influencers in your niche:** active and from competitors.
* **Easy to use:** Create successful campaigns even without prior experience.
* **Increase sales:** Boost your sales with targeted strategies.
* **Detailed analysis:** Track the performance of your campaigns with precise metrics.

Transform your marketing strategy and see the results in your store. Start today with **Viral**!

= Connection with viral.com.br =

You must connect your store to [viral.com.br](https://viral.com.br) to automatically generate personalized discount coupons for each influencer, in addition to receiving sales metrics from the periods of your campaigns with influencers. The connection gives Viral permission to read/write discount coupons and also to read orders. If you want to know more about how Viral uses collected data, access our [Privacy Policy](https://viral.com.br/en/privacy-policy) and our [Terms of Service](https://viral.com.br/en/terms-of-service)

== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* PHP 7.4 or higher is required (PHP 8.0 or higher is recommended)

= Automatic Installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option -- WordPress will handle everything, and you won’t need to leave your web browser. To perform an automatic installation of the Viral plugin, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu, and click "Add New".

In the search field, type "Viral" and press enter. Once you’ve found the plugin, you can install it by clicking "Install Now", and WordPress will take it from there.

= Manual Installation =

Manual installation requires downloading the Viral plugin and uploading it to your web server via your favorite FTP application. WordPress provides [instructions on how to do this here](https://wordpress.org/support/article/managing-plugins/#manual-plugin-installation).

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What happens after installation? =

You will be redirected to https://viral.com.br, where the process to connect to your WooCommerce will begin automatically. Then, you will be redirected to the confirmation screen of your WooCommerce, giving permission for us to create and read the coupons from your store and permission to read orders to create the sales metric and track the performance of your influencers.
